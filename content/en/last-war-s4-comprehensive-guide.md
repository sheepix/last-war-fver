# Last War S4 Comprehensive Guide: Evernight Isle Mechanics, Formations, and Advanced Tips

> Last Modified: 2025-03-21
> Applicable Season: Season 4 - Evernight Isle

This is the complete guide for *Last War: Survival* Season 4 (S4 - Evernight Isle), covering new mechanic explanations, hero recommendations, formation strategies, building upgrade priorities, weekly key points, and season transition tips.

---

## 1. S4 Core New Mechanics Overview

### 🌙 Season Theme: Evernight Isle

Season 4 introduces the **Night Mechanic** - the entire map is shrouded in darkness and requires **Lighthouses** to dispel it.

### 🔑 New Core Systems

| System | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Lighthouse** | Dispels darkness, provides light buffs and virus resistance |
| **Generator** | Powers lighthouses (up to 4 personal generators + 1 alliance generator) |
| **Electrician** | New assistant that provides illumination for troops and removes Blood Night zombie buffs |
| **Optoelectronics Lab** | Increases virus resistance, unlocks generators and electricians |
| **Quartz Workshop** | Produces quartz (up to 4, weekly ticket +1 extra) |
| **Tactical Cards** | New combat system with 2 core cards + 8 extra cards |
| **Magatama Store** | Exchange magatama for quartz, tactical card packs, etc. |

### ⚡ Power Management System

The core gameplay of S4 revolves around **power management**:

- **Resource Chain**: Stone → Quartz Workshop → Quartz → Optoelectronics Lab → Virus Resistance → Combat Effectiveness
- **During Blood Night**: Consumes 18,000 power per minute, most players' reserves will deplete
- **Tip**: Be sure to enable **auto-adjustment settings** to prevent lights from going out when power runs out

## 3. S4 Building Upgrade Priority

### 🏗️ Day 1 Immediate Actions

**Upgrade Order (Very Important)**:

1. **Kill Lv.190 Doomsday Walker** → Get first-kill reward of 207K stone
2. **Kill Lv.1-3 Small Zombies** → Get first-kill rewards (no resistance needed)
3. **Occupy Excavation Site** → Get stone per hour

### 📋 Building Priority List

| Priority | Building | Target Level | Notes |
|----------|----------|--------------|-------|
| 🥇 | Optoelectronics Lab | Level 35 | Unlocks more generators and electricians, highest season priority |
| 🥈 | Lighthouse | Level 10→35 | Uses only iron and food, needs Optoelectronics Lab at 35 to continue past 10 |
| 🥉 | Quartz Workshop | Level 15+ | Build from level 15, each level increases cost but same output |
| ⭐ | Alliance Center | Follow HQ | Help/reinforcement capacity |
| ⭐ | Hospital | Level 12+ | Save 70%+ troops |

### ⚠️ Important Tips

- Build Quartz Workshops from level 15 since each level increases cost but output remains the same
- Lighthouse can only continue upgrading after Optoelectronics Lab reaches level 35
- Equip **Electrician** to your first troop to remove Blood Night zombie buffs

## 4. Blood Night Event Guide

### 🌙 Blood Night Schedule (3 times daily, 30 minutes each)

| Time | Description |
|------|-------------|
| 02:30 | Server Time |
| 10:30 | Server Time |
| 18:30 | Server Time |

### Blood Night Rules

- Zombies receive **50% buff**, becoming stronger
- Lighthouse power demand increases dramatically
- **Electrician troops** can light the way and remove zombie buffs
- Bases may be attacked by wandering zombies in the dark
- Higher chance to get tactical card packs from defeating zombies
- Earn **Magatama** (personal + alliance objectives)

### 🎯 Magatama Store Exchange Priority

Refreshes weekly, be sure to exchange:

1. ✅ **Quartz** (highest priority)
2. ✅ Tactical SR/UR Card Packs
3. ✅ Overlord Training Certificates and Supply Crates
4. ✅ Lucky Cat Decoration (from Week 2)

## 5. Tactical Card System

### 🃏 Card Configuration

- **2 Core Cards** (contain special tactical abilities, UR is extremely rare)
- **8 Extra Cards** (4 combat + 4 resource)

### Acquisition Methods

- Defeating Blood Night zombies
- Purchasing from Magatama Store
- Maximum 20 new cards per day

### 💡 F2P Card Strategy

- Keep each card type until needed or you get matching cards
- Dismantle duplicate cards, use fragments to upgrade other cards
- Prioritize upgrading core cards

### Recommended Deck (General Configuration)

**Main Setup**:
- Windrusher
- Rapid March
- Damage Reduction Reversal or Damage Reversal
- Mixed Formation

## 7. Recommended Formations Explained

### Formation 1: 4 Tanks + 1 Adam Mixed (F2P/Low Spender Recommended)

Utilize season cards to achieve cross-faction bonuses.

**Formation Composition**:
- Frontline: **Scarlett** + **Kimberly**
- Backline: **Adam** + **Marshall** + **Murphy**

**Core Mechanics**:
- Adam provides "Defensive Counterattack" status to frontline, triggering normal attack counterattack when hit
- Scarlett has extremely high normal attack multiplier (up to 1400% physical damage), combined with counterattack for explosive output
- Marshall locks onto enemy highest attack unit, assisting with focused fire

**Advantages**:
- Counters aircraft teams (Scarlett deals physical damage, ignores energy damage reduction)
- High strength at same power level, can suppress across power levels
- F2P-friendly, heroes relatively easy to acquire

---

### Formation 2: Traditional Aircraft Team (Air Squad)

**Core Heroes**: DVA + Lucius + Kali + Skyler + Morrison

**Advantages**:
- High energy damage reduction, advantage against skill damage
- Strong burst capability, quickly kills squishy targets

**Disadvantages**:
- Countered by physical damage formations (like Scarlett tank teams)

---

### Formation 3: Tank Main Formation (S4 Recommended)

Tank formations receive 25% same-type unit buff in S4, very powerful.

**Formation**: Williams + Murphy + Kimberly + Tesla + Sturman

**Note**: 3-4 tanks form the optimal setup, very strong with season mechanics.

## 8. S4 Weekly Guide Key Points

### 📅 Week 1: Foundation Building

**Unlocked Content**:
- Optoelectronics Lab, Quartz Workshop, Alliance Center
- Tactical Cards, Lighthouse
- Blood Night arrival (preparation phase)
- Clearance Operations (first-kill rewards)

**Key Tasks**:
- Kill Lv.190 Doomsday Walker for stone
- Occupy excavation sites
- Upgrade Lighthouse to level 10
- Kill Lv.1-3 small zombies for first-kill rewards

### 📅 Week 2: Blood Night Hunters

**Unlocked Content**:
- Blood Night Hunters PvP event
- Food Sanctuary
- Divine Tree Blessing
- Demon Patrol Vehicle

**Blood Night Hunters Rules**:
- Requires Blood Night Hunter profession skill (approx. level 70)
- Hunters receive: +250 virus resistance, +20% morale, +10% march speed
- Hunters can attack each other
- Automatically enters War Frenzy state (cannot use shield)

### 📅 Week 3: Hero Upgrades and Faction Selection

**Unlocked Content**:
- Sarah UR upgrade
- Trade War
- **Faction Selection** (Shadow Village vs. Armor Unit)

**Faction Selection Tips**:
- Two factions: Shadow Village and Armor Unit
- Each faction has 4 war zones
- Affects subsequent Copper War declaration order
- Be sure to coordinate selection with your alliance

### 📅 Week 4+: Copper War

**Core Objective**: Collect as much **Copper** as possible

- Get copper per hour by occupying cities
- Used to upgrade Alliance Center
- Faction duels determine final season ranking
- Total 8 rounds (2 rounds per week)

**Copper War Skills**:
- **Attacker**: Warlord's Atomic Breath
- **Defender**: Butler's Tesla Coil

## 9. S3 to S4 Transition Tips

### Pre-Transition Preparation (Late S3)

1. **Stockpile stamina** - Need lots of stamina to kill world zombies and participate in seasonal events
2. **Save radar missions** - Complete quickly at season start
3. **Stop killing Doomsday Walkers** - Stop 1-2 days before season, wait for S4 first-kill rewards
4. **Save hero return tickets** - Use in early S4
5. **Buy purple fragments** - Prepare for Sarah UR upgrade
6. **Buy current season base skin** - Use its buff in S4

### After S4 Opens (Day 1-7)

1. **Don't kill Doomsday Walkers immediately** - Wait for season to start for first-kill rewards
2. **Prioritize getting Optoelectronics Lab to level 35**
3. **Unlock Quartz Workshops** - Start the stone→quartz chain
4. **Enable Blood Night auto-adjustment settings**
5. **Kill Lv.1-3 small zombies** - Get first-kill rewards without resistance
6. **Occupy excavation sites ASAP** - Provide hourly resources for all alliance members
7. **Kill level 190 Doomsday Walkers daily**

## 10. F2P Player Daily Optimization

### ⏰ Daily Must-Do

| Time | Action |
|------|--------|
| Every 4 hours | Log in to collect stamina and resources |
| Blood Night times | Participate in Blood Night events, defeat zombies for magatama |
| Daily | Complete all daily quests |
| Alliance | Participate in all alliance activities |

### 💡 Efficiency Tips

**Alliance Coordination**:
- Low-resistance allies initiate rallies, high-resistance allies join to complete
- Dispatch electricians to each other to boost light status
- Daily dispatch electricians to charge the Divine Tree

**Resource Management**:
- Prioritize alliance tech "Veteran Artisan" for all seasonal buildings (reduces build cost)
- Start with Engineer profession (reduces build and research costs)
- Batch train troops, utilize bonus events

**Event Participation**:
- Only participate in free tiers and achievable objectives
- Hoard tokens, wait for reward bonus events
- Prioritize completing alliance activities for team rewards
